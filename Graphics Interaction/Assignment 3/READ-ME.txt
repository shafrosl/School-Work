CONTROLS
0 - Program takes a screen capture and saves it into image file
9 - takes the above screen capture and uses it as painting in the room

W - move camera forward
S - move camera backwards
A - move camera left
D - move camera right

Right Click and Drag to rotate camera

Esc - exit

Able to:
- change position of the light
- change colour and reflectivity of Cube
- change colour of glass partition
- overall reflection of objects in the room
