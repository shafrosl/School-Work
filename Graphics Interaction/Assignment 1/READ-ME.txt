CONTROLS
1 - Front View
2 - Top Down View
0 - Resets poisition of cameras

W - move camera forward
S - move camera backwards
A - move camera left
D - move camera right

R - randomize planet movement, rings, speed
P - stops/resumes movement

Right Click and Drag to rotate camera

Esc - exit
