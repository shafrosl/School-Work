CONTROLS
V - Able too change between perspective view and multiple views

W - move camera forward
S - move camera backwards
A - move camera left
D - move camera right

Right Click and Drag to rotate camera only for view 4 (bottom right corner) and in perspective view

Esc - exit

Able to:
- view wireframe
- toggle disco mode
- reset items back into primary position
- switch lights on/off
- change ambient/specular/diffusion/attenuation of lights
- randomize colour of items on reload of program (exit and reopen program)
